User Journey Map

This journey map illustrates a typical interaction flow for users of the Health Companion app. It is based on the needs, goals, and pain points identified in the personas of Ryan and Helen. The journey emphasizes low-effort interactions, clarity, and reliability.

1. Onboarding & Setup

User Actions

Downloads the app, sets medication schedules, reviews upcoming appointments, and adjusts accessibility preferences.

Touchpoints

Welcome screen, medication setup flow, accessibility settings.

Emotions

Ryan feels cautiously optimistic. Helen feels slightly anxious but reassured by a clear interface.

Pain Points

Too many steps, small text, or excessive data entry could feel overwhelming.

2. Receiving a Reminder

User Actions

Receives a medication reminder via push notification and opens the app if needed.

Touchpoints

Push notifications, lock screen alerts.

Emotions

Reassured and supported. Ryan feels relieved, Helen feels confident she will not forget.

Pain Points

Missed notifications or reminders that disappear too quickly.

3. Confirming Medication

User Actions

Confirms medication intake with a single tap.

Touchpoints

Confirmation screen with a large primary action button.

Emotions

Calm and satisfied. Users feel a sense of accomplishment.

Pain Points

Accidental taps or fear of making irreversible mistakes.

4. Reviewing Progress & Appointments

User Actions

Checks medication streaks and views upcoming appointments from the home screen.

Touchpoints

Home dashboard, progress indicators, appointments list.

Emotions

Encouraged and reassured. Ryan feels motivated, Helen feels in control.

Pain Points

Overly complex charts or cluttered information displays.

5. Long-Term Use

User Actions

Relies on reminders long-term with minimal interaction and occasional setting adjustments.

Touchpoints

Recurring notifications, settings screen.

Emotions

Trust and dependability. The app fades into the background of daily life.

Pain Points

Unexpected UI changes or increasing interaction requirements over time.
